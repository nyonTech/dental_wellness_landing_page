Teeth Whitening Options At Dental Wellness

ZOOM/ In-office Teeth Whitening

Tooth discoloration may result from drinking coffee, tea, cola and red wine; or from smoking. The aging process also can stain and darken your teeth. You may also have naturally yellow or greyish teeth because of genetic factors.

Zoom is the number one world-wide patient requested professional teeth whitening brand.

The process involves the use of the Philips ZOOM light to activate the components of the bleach that is placed directly onto the teeth. The procedure takes an hour and you will leave the office with a noticeably whiter smile, upto 8 shades whiter than the one prior to treatment.The procedure is safe, reliable and hassle free.

At-home teeth whitening kit

At Dental Wellness we offer custom take home whitening kits. Custom trays are made after taking an impression of your teeth which will help to hold the whitening gel in place and make sure all the areas of your teeth are whitened. A small amount of the bleaching gel has to be dispensed into the clear trays and worn for up to four hours a day for one to two weeks. After this, an occasional single use now and then is all you’ll need to keep your teeth looking their best.

Over the counter whitening trays can move around and aren’t able to bend and curve around the anatomy of your teeth. Tooth-whitening gel can leak out of ill-fitting trays causing irritation to your gums, as well as not whitening as efficiently as custom trays. With trays that specifically fit your mouth and bite, you can avoid these problems.
